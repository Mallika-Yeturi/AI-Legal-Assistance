Employment Contract

This Employment Contract ("Contract") is entered into on this day, ____________________, between Tern, hereinafter referred to as "Employer," and Goutham, hereinafter referred to as "Employee."

1. Position:
Employee shall be employed in the position of testing.

2. Salary:
Employee shall receive a salary of $30,000 per year, payable in regular installments on a bi-weekly basis.

3. Duration:
This Contract shall be effective for a period of 1 year, commencing on June 23, 2025, and ending on June 22, 2026.

4. Probation Period:
Employee shall be subject to a probationary period of 20 days, during which time either party may terminate this Contract with or without cause.

5. Duties and Responsibilities:
Employee shall perform all duties and responsibilities associated with the position of testing, as assigned by Employer.

6. Confidentiality:
Employee agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information and trade secrets of Employer, both during and after the term of this Contract.

7. Non-Compete:
Employee agrees not to engage in any competitive activities that may harm the business interests of Employer during the term of this Contract and for a period of 1 year after its termination.

8. Governing Law:
This Contract shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.
